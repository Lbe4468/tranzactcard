Tranzact-Up

Each time you make a purchase with the TranzactCard, the transaction amount is rounded up to the nearest dollar, and the difference is automatically deposited into your TZT Account.


Tranzact-Back

Better than cashback! The TranzactCard is automatically linked to a cashback system that includes nearly half a million merchants nationwide. Each time you use your TranzactCard at one of these merchants the cashback percentage is automatically deposited into your TZT Account.
