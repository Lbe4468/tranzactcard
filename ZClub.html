The Z-Club is filled with quality products that TranzactCard sources directly. Because TranzactCard is a closed-loop, member-only environment we are able to obtain thousands upon thousands of products at wholesale cost. The products are given a floor price - which is set competitively above wholesale cost - and listed in the Z-Club at MSRP. You can then use your Z-Bucks to buy down the price of the product as much as you want, all the way down to the floor price. And it gets even better! TranzactCard offers Flash Sales, where specific products will be available to purchase with Z-Bucks only.

Imagine being able to have a brand new iPhone using only your Z-Bucks?! This true dollar-for-dollar program will double your buying power, saving you tons of money. TranzactCard also matches a percentage of each purchase in the Z-Club and deposits the amount into your TZT account.
